# Personal Trainer — Protein & Workout Tracker

A focused fitness app built around one goal: more muscle, less body fat. Removes the cognitive load of knowing what to eat and what to do each day.

**[Open the app](https://jdgyp.github.io/personal-trainer)**

## What it does

- **Protein tracker** — log meals with one tap, gamified progress ring with daily streak counter
- **Daily workout** — shows exactly which exercises to do today based on your training schedule (3–5 days per week), with sets and reps
- **Knee-aware** — flags exercises that stress the knee joint so you can modify if needed
- **Progress view** — 7-day protein history showing which days you hit your goal
- **Fully customisable** — set your protein goal, training days, and quick-log shortcuts in settings

## How to use

1. Open Settings — set your daily protein goal and which days you train
2. Log protein throughout the day using quick shortcuts or custom entries
3. Check off exercises as you complete them

No account needed. All data saves in your browser.

## Built with

Single HTML file — no frameworks, no dependencies, no build step. Built with [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code).
